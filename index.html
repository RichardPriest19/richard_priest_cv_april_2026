Career Highlights

🛡️
Delivered Bowman tactical radio conversion programme IT support for 80,000 British Army vehicles at MOD Ashchurch — In-Service Declaration achieved March 2004, described by the MoD as unprecedented in UK defence acquisition history.
⚡
Led IT/OT convergence strategy at EDF Renewables UK & Ireland, overseeing SCADA integration, asset performance management, and cyber security across onshore wind, offshore wind, solar, and BESS portfolios targeting 10 GW by 2035.
🌿
Served as IT Director at Nova Pangaea Technologies during a $29 million funding round, supporting development of NOVAONE — the UK's first second-generation bioethanol commercial plant within the Teesside Freeport zone under Project Speedbird with British Airways.
🏆
Played a key role in Bidwells' website and digital transformation programme, contributing to the firm winning Best New Website at the Property Marketing Awards 2022.
🌐
Held regional IT Director accountability for HID Global across UK, EMEA, and Asia Pacific — managing infrastructure across data centres, WAN, and enterprise connectivity in over 30 countries, with ISO 27001 compliance and M&A integration leadership.
📈
Supported Missguided's rapid scale-up toward $80M+ revenue, managing e-commerce platform stability during high-traffic celebrity launches including Nicole Scherzinger (March 2014) and enabling international expansion into the USA, Europe, and Australia.
